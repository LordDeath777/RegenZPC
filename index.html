Dedication to Customer Service

With over a decade of experience in building the world’s most advanced custom gaming computers, servers, and premium parts. Each PC is hand built by skilled technicians. Everything from inventory allocation, assembly, testing, and quality assurance are done in-house to ensure we provide only the very best for our customers. It’s what makes owning a PC, lapotop, or server truly special, being handmade with perfection and finished with utmost care.

High-performance hardware needs to be properly tested for bottlenecks and stability issues. Our team of technicians test every custom gaming computer, server, or laptop with a series of stress-tests and benchmarks that analyze the processor, memory, graphics cards, storage, and even the power supply. Once the devices pass these tests, a second technician verifies them before sending the computer, server, or parts to our quality assurance team.

Putting together a custom gaming pc, server, or laptop is only part of the process, our expert technicians are obsessed with squeezing every ounce of performance from our computers. To unlock maximum performance, factors such as: the wiring job, chassis airflow, power delivery, and layout of components represent the core foundation of a true custom PC.

Every purchase comes with lifetime expert support by our in-house technicians. Our team strives to resolve any issue with your device or part as quickly and efficiently as possible. As a commitment to our dedication in helping our customers, RegenZPC is rated an A+ from the BBB for over 10+ years since we’ve been in business. We imagine the situation from the customer’s shoes, and treat them just like we would want ourselves to be treated!
